About Me

Hello, I'm Adum Sekar Fatimah Azzaro. I am an undergraduate student from Psychology, at Yogyakarta State University. During my undergraduate education, I was interested in several fields that made me happy and excited to be in that field.

In addition, I am also interested in the world of photography. This world is unique because apart from honing skills, I also use them as coping with stress.
